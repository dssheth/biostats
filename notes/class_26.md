### Non- Parametric
- can't measure when not normally distributed
- nominal(names), ordinal(ranks) data do not have means
- data normally dist., but when all the three normalising are failing
    - shapiro fail in low sample size,when low data points
    - robust for normality, t-test, anova
    if small data set, can use shapiro but not sure about the results
    - can have skewness in the data, use non para.
    mean of all data points including the skewed values, outliers


In parametric, use everything from data, in Non-para. check median and mode, not affected by outliers
- with data also lose statistical power.
- data points
